# What Are IO Games? Top 10 Free Browser Multiplayer Games

IO games are browser-based multiplayer games that took the internet by storm. Simple to learn, hard to put down, and free to play.

## What Makes IO Games Special?

- **Instant play** — No download, no sign-up, just click and play
- **Real-time multiplayer** — Compete against players worldwide
- **Simple mechanics** — Easy to learn, hard to master
- **Short sessions** — Perfect for quick gaming breaks

## Top IO Games on LeYou

### Snake.io
The classic snake game goes multiplayer. Eat food, grow longer, and compete against other players in real-time. [Play Snake.io](/en/games/snake-io)

### Hole.io
Control a black hole and consume everything in your path. Start small and grow massive by swallowing buildings, cars, and more. [Play Hole.io](/en/games/hole-io)

## Why IO Games Work

IO games succeed because they respect your time. A match lasts 2-5 minutes. There's no commitment, no download, no barrier to entry. Just fun.
