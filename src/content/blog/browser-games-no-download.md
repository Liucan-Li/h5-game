# Why Browser Games Are Making a Comeback in 2026

Browser-based games are experiencing a renaissance. After years of being overshadowed by mobile apps and console titles, HTML5 games are back — and bigger than ever.

## The Numbers Don't Lie

Platforms like Poki (200M+ monthly active users) and CrazyGames prove there's enormous demand for instant-play browser games. Players are rediscovering the joy of clicking a link and playing immediately.

## Why Players Are Returning

### Zero Friction
No download, no install, no update, no storage space needed. You click, you play. This simplicity is powerful.

### Cross-Platform by Default
Your save follows you. Your browser knows you. Play on any device, anywhere.

### School and Office Friendly
Browser games work on school Chromebooks, work laptops, and restricted devices where installing software isn't an option.

### Social and Shareable
IO games and daily challenges are easy to share. Send a link, your friend plays instantly.

## The Future

With WebGL and WebAssembly, browser games now rival native apps in performance. The gap is closing fast.
