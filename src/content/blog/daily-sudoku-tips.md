# Daily Sudoku Tips and Strategies for Beginners

Sudoku is one of the most popular logic puzzles in the world. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your solving speed, these strategies will help.

## Basic Techniques

### Scanning
Start by scanning rows and columns. Look for numbers that appear frequently — they give you the most clues about where to place missing numbers.

### Cross-Hatching
Pick a number and a 3x3 box. Look at the row and column constraints to narrow down where that number must go. This is the most fundamental Sudoku technique.

### Pencil Marks
When you can't solve a cell immediately, note possible candidates lightly (pencil marks). This helps you spot patterns that aren't obvious at first glance.
