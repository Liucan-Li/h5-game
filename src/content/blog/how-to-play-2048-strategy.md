# How to Play 2048: Strategy Guide to Reach the 2048 Tile Every Time

2048 is deceptively simple — slide tiles, combine matching numbers, reach 2048. But winning consistently requires strategy. Here's how.

## The Corner Strategy

This is the most reliable technique for reaching 2048:

1. **Pick a corner** (bottom-left is most common) and keep your highest tile there
2. **Never move that tile** away from the corner
3. **Build in two directions** — only use the two arrows that push toward your corner (e.g., left and down)
4. **Clear smaller numbers first** — don't let low-value tiles block your corner

## Step-by-Step

### Early Game (2s, 4s, 8s)
Keep tiles organized. Don't rush combinations. Make tidy merges.

### Mid Game (16s, 32s, 64s)
Your corner tile should be your largest. Build a "chain" along the edge with decreasing values.

### Late Game (128s, 256s, 512s)
Patience is everything. One wrong move can break your chain. Take your time and plan ahead.

## Common Mistakes

- Moving the corner tile away from the corner
- Using all four directions (stick to two!)
- Merging too quickly without planning the chain
