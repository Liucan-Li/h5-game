# Best Free Card Games to Play Online in Your Browser

Card games have been entertaining people for centuries. Here are the best free card games you can play online right now — no download, no sign-up.

## Solitaire (Klondike)

The classic patience game. Sort all cards by suit from Ace to King. Timeless, relaxing, and surprisingly strategic. [Play Solitaire](/en/games/solitaire)

## Blackjack

Beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. The world's most popular casino card game, now in your browser. [Play Blackjack](/en/games/blackjack)

## Texas Hold'em

The world's most popular poker variant. Use your hole cards and community cards to build the best five-card hand. Play against AI and sharpen your poker skills. [Play Texas Hold'em](/en/games/texas-holdem)

## War

The simplest card game — both players flip a card simultaneously, higher card wins. Fast, fun, and unpredictable. [Play War](/en/games/war-card)
