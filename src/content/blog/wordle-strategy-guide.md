# How to Win at Wordle Every Time — 5 Expert Strategies

Wordle is the daily word puzzle that took the world by storm. Here are 5 strategies to improve your game.

## 1. Start with a Strong Opening Word
Words like "STARE", "CRANE", or "AUDIO" cover the most common letters. A good start narrows possibilities fast.

## 2. Use Process of Elimination
Your first 2-3 guesses should cover as many common consonants and vowels as possible. Don't try to solve on guess 1.

## 3. Pay Attention to Letter Position
Green means correct letter in the correct position. Yellow means correct letter, wrong position. Use this information systematically.

## 4. Keep a Mental Letter Bank
Track which letters you've eliminated. Don't reuse known-wrong letters.

## 5. Practice Daily
The best way to improve is daily practice. Play LeYou's [Daily Wordle](/en/daily/wordle) every day to build your skills.
